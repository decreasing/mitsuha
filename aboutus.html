ABOUT US



The idea of SilverScreen all started around our college years from a project that a professor assigned to us. It was create simple website while giving us the independence of choosing the content of it. Our team at SilverScreen are die-hard movie and tv series enthusiasts then until now and we arrived to a conclusion in creating a movie review or critic site. From that simple and humble beginning, we came to an agreement to try and improving it. Throughout the years of perserverance, we eventually made SilverScreen what is today.

At SilverScreen our goal is to help all people from regular movie watchers to serious critics whether to consider watching a newly released movie or an old one that they haven't watched yet. Doing movie reviews is a very crucial process as it impacts not only the success of the movie but the whole production and crew behind it. This means criticizing the people responsible for creating the movie.

We take movie reviewing as our pride and joy and we are still continuing our journey to build up our community as movies unite people and create memories.
